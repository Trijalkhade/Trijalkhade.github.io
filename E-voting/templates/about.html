What is e-Voting?

The e-Voting System is a secure and user-friendly online platform that allows citizens to cast their votes in elections from the comfort of their homes or any location with internet access.

Core Objectives

  • Ensure security and integrity in online voting.
  • Enable ease of access for all users.
  • Provide transparency and real-time updates for administrators.

System Workflow

  1. Home Page: The starting point of the system. It introduces users to the platform and provides options to log in or register.
  2. Login Page: Secure user login for identity verification. Only authenticated users can vote.
  3. Voting Page: Displays a list of candidates along with their party names and symbols. Voters can select their preferred candidate.
  4. Candidate Numbers: Each candidate is assigned a unique number for easy identification.
  5. Voting Success Page: A confirmation screen that assures users their vote has been successfully recorded.
  6. Result Page: After voting ends, this page shows a transparent tally of all votes per candidate.
  7. Admin Dashboard: Accessible only to administrators. It includes real-time data on voting activity, candidate performance, and system management tools.
